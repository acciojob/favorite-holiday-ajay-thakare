Paris, France


Paris, the City of Light, is one of the most popular holiday destinations in the world. Known for its rich history, stunning architecture, romantic atmosphere, and world-class cuisine, Paris offers an unforgettable travel experience for visitors from around the globe.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The iconic symbol of Paris and a must-see landmark.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to world-famous artworks like the Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Seine River Cruise – A romantic way to view the city’s illuminated landmarks.
  5. Champs-Élysées – A vibrant avenue known for shopping and cafés.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – Flaky and buttery breakfast pastries.
  • Escargots – A traditional French delicacy of cooked snails with garlic butter.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes served sweet or savory.
  • Macarons – Colorful and delicate almond-based cookies.
  • French Onion Soup – A hearty dish topped with melted cheese.
